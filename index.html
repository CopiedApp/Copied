How It Works

3 Easy Steps

Install the App

Download the Android app from playstore, create an account. Download the same for your computer variant & use the same email address

Start Copying

On Android, after copying an item, you can choose whether it should be copied or not. On your computer, no extra action is needed

Enjoy The Magic

Your copied item will be synced on the cloud & will be available for pasting in all of the supported operating systems; Android, Mac, Linux & Windows
